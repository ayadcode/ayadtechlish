Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Get answers to the most common questions about our English course and learning process.

You can start at any level! I accept students from complete beginners to advanced learners. During our initial assessment, I'll evaluate your current level and create a personalized learning plan that matches your abilities and goals. Whether you're just starting to learn English or looking to polish your advanced skills, I'll tailor the course to your needs.

All lessons are conducted online via video call (Zoom, Skype, or your preferred platform). Each session is 60 minutes and includes interactive activities, conversation practice, grammar instruction, and personalized feedback. I use digital whiteboards, multimedia resources, and engaging materials to make every lesson dynamic and effective. You'll also receive homework assignments and progress reports to track your improvement.

All study materials are included in the course fee! I provide digital textbooks, workbooks, audio files, practice exercises, and supplementary resources tailored to your level and goals. You'll just need a computer/tablet with internet connection, a notebook for taking notes, and a headset or microphone for clear audio during lessons. Everything else is provided by me.

Yes, I understand that life can be unpredictable! You can reschedule lessons with at least 24 hours notice without any penalty. I offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to accommodate different time zones and work schedules. If you need to cancel with less than 24 hours notice, we can discuss makeup options based on your specific circumstances.

Most students notice improvements within the first 2-3 weeks, especially in confidence and speaking fluency. Significant progress typically occurs within 4-6 weeks with consistent practice. Your improvement speed depends on factors like your starting level, lesson frequency, and time spent practicing between lessons. I provide weekly progress reports so you can track your advancement every step of the way.

I provide exam-specific strategies, practice tests, timing techniques, and detailed feedback on all four skills (reading, writing, listening, speaking). We'll work on your weak areas and develop test-taking strategies to maximize your score potential.
